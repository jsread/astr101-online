# About the course

This course is developed by the [Department of Physics at California State University Fullerton](https://physics.fullerton.edu/). It draws on lecture slides co-developed by Astronomy professors in the department, many of who participated in teacher training with the [Center for Astronomy Education](https://www.as.arizona.edu/cae).
