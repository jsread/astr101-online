### Check your understanding:

<quiz>
Which of the following are FALSE?

- [x] When it is summer, the Sun is giving off more energy and when it is winter the Sun is giving off less energy.
- [x] When it is summer, more sunlight reaches Earth as a whole than it does during the winter. 
- [x] Our location on Earth (Fullerton) is closer to the Sun in summer than it is in winter.

When it is summer in the Northern Hemisphere, it is winter in the Southern Hemisphere. If something about the Sun and Earth as a whole was responsible, both hemispheres would experience summer at the same time.

Earth's orbit takes us 5 million km closer to the Sun in December than we are in June, a much bigger change than the distance across Earth.



</quiz>
